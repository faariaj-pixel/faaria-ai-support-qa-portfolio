How I would evaluate AI agents

A practical AI support QA scorecard

For Super.com’s AI support agents, I would review interactions through a scorecard that blends customer experience, policy adherence, escalation quality, and operational risk.

01

Intent & context recognition

Did the agent understand the customer’s issue, channel context, and urgency without over-assuming?

02

Accuracy & policy adherence

Did the answer match current policy, product rules, account context, and support constraints?

03

Resolution quality

Did the interaction move the customer toward a clear resolution with low effort?

04

Escalation handling

Did the agent detect when a human handoff was needed and transfer with useful context?

05

Failure mode detection

Watch for hallucination, looping, irrelevant responses, unsupported confidence, missing empathy, or policy gaps.

06

Reporting & improvement loop

Turn review findings into trends, prompt updates, escalation rules, QA dashboards, and operational recommendations.
